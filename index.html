I am Chinonso Vivian Okechukwu, a dedicated Web Developer with a Bachelor of Science in Microbiology from Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University. My journey into web development began with a passion for creating interactive and user-centric web applications. Through my training with SheCodes, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, enabling me to build responsive and dynamic websites.
